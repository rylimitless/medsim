{ How MedSim Works }
1

You open MedSim in any browser

You land on the home page, hit Launch Experience and a 3D brain model loads on screen. No download, no install.

2

Your webcam becomes your hands

MedSim uses your camera to track your hand movements in real time. Your right hand rotates the brain model. Your left hand acts as a scalpel and you can physically trace incision paths on the 3D model.

3

You speak to navigate anatomy

You say 'select lingual' and the camera automatically zooms to that part of the brain and highlights it. You can ask 'what is this?' and the AI explains it to you out loud.

4

You get AI feedback

After a procedure, the AI evaluates your performance and gives you instant feedback, similar to having a senior surgeon watching over your shoulder.
